Before you begin

  • Answer based on how you actually behave, not how you wish you did. There are no correct answers.
  • Your first instinct is almost always the most accurate. Do not overthink your responses.
  • All four DISC styles are equally valid. This assessment measures preferences, not ability or intelligence.
  • Section G asks how you behave when fully relaxed with no professional role to play. Answer honestly.
  • Your results and a personalised report will be generated automatically when you finish.
